# Compound-Interest-Calculator
See exactly how your money can grow over time. Enter a starting amount, set a monthly contribution, choose your expected interest rate, and watch compound interest do its work — across 150+ world currencies.
Whether you're planning for retirement, building an emergency fund, or just curious about the power of long-term saving, this calculator breaks down how much of your final balance comes from what you put in versus what your money earns on its own. Adjust the sliders to explore different scenarios instantly.
